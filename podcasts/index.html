HR Talks Series

Practical HR conversations for leaders making hard people decisions.

HR Talks is HM Pinnacle’s conversation series on the moments leaders usually avoid until they become urgent: terminations, layoffs, recruiting, leadership behavior, vendor relationships, workplace dynamics, and HR crisis response. The goal is simple: make these decisions clearer, cleaner, and more human.

Why This Series Matters

Where HR gets real

These episodes are built for the leaders who do not have time for abstract theory. Every conversation is grounded in the practical, messy realities of managing people well while still protecting the business.

Clearer Leadership Language

Heather and Cara model the kind of direct language leaders need when the stakes are high and ambiguity creates downstream risk.

People Ops Execution

Every episode ties people decisions back to process, documentation, accountability, and the systems that keep teams stable as they grow.

High-Trust Decision Making

The series balances empathy and structure, showing how to protect dignity without creating confusion, drift, or avoidable business risk.
